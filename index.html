The Second Coming, commonly nicknamed Orange or abbreviated to TSC, (described as The Chosen One's Return,) is the main protagonist of the Animator vs. Animation franchise.

The Chosen One

NO ONE, formerly known as The Chosen One (tentatively known as victim, killer, and BEAST), is one of the tritagonists of the Animator vs. Animation franchise. He was created after victim managed to escape from noogai3's computer and the first of the young animator's creations to prove himself a genuine threat due to possessing superpowers thanks to his abnormally high power level granted to him by his creator.

The Dark Lord

The Dark Lord (tentatively known and can be described as The Chosen One's enemy), also called TDL, is the main antagonist of the Animator vs. Animation franchise. Being the most dangerous villain in the series, he serves as a major character in Animator vs. Animation Season 1, the main antagonist of Animator vs. Animation Season 2 and the overarching antagonist of Animator vs. Animation Season 3.

Victim

H4CK3R, formerly known as victim, is the secondary antagonist of the Animator vs. Animation franchise and the main antagonist of Animator vs. Animation Season 3. He is the first stick figure created by noogai3 and the first stick figure of the franchise in its entirety.
